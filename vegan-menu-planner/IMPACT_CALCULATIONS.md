# Impact Metrics Calculations - Fermi Estimates

## Methodology

### Carbon Footprint Calculations
Based on research data:
- **Beef**: ~27 kg CO2e per kg
- **Pork**: ~12 kg CO2e per kg  
- **Chicken**: ~6.9 kg CO2e per kg
- **Turkey**: ~10.9 kg CO2e per kg
- **Fish (average)**: ~5.5 kg CO2e per kg
- **Dairy (butter, cream)**: ~13.5 kg CO2e per kg
- **Eggs**: ~4.8 kg CO2e per dozen

- **Vegan alternatives**: ~2 kg CO2e per kg (average for plant-based proteins)

### Animals Spared Calculations
Based on typical serving sizes and animal yields:
- **Chicken**: 1.8 kg dressed weight → ~6 servings of 100g each → 0.17 chickens per serving
- **Turkey**: 7 kg dressed weight → ~35 servings of 100g each → 0.03 turkeys per serving
- **Beef**: 220 kg from one cow → ~2200 servings of 100g each → 0.0005 cows per serving
- **Pork**: 90 kg from one pig → ~900 servings of 100g each → 0.001 pigs per serving
- **Fish**: 0.5 kg per fish (average) → ~5 servings of 100g each → 0.2 fish per serving

## Event-Specific Calculations (100 people)

### Thanksgiving Dinner

#### Menu 1: Traditional Harvest Feast ($22/person)
**Dishes Analysis:**
- Herb-Roasted Tofurky (replaces turkey): 100 servings × 150g = 15kg turkey equivalent
- Green Bean Casserole (replaces version with bacon/butter): 100 servings × 30g dairy = 3kg dairy
- Pumpkin Pie (replaces eggs/dairy): 100 servings × 20g dairy + 10g eggs = 2kg dairy + 1kg eggs

**Carbon:**
- Turkey saved: 15kg × 10.9 = 163.5 kg CO2e
- Dairy saved: 5kg × 13.5 = 67.5 kg CO2e
- Eggs saved: 1kg × 4.8 = 4.8 kg CO2e
- Vegan alternative: 15kg × 2 = 30 kg CO2e
- **Net savings: 205.8 kg CO2e**

**Animals:**
- Turkeys: 15kg ÷ 7kg per turkey = 2.1 → **2 turkeys**
- Chickens (for stock/gravy): 100 servings × 0.05 = **5 chickens**
- **Total: 7 animals**
